Photo & Video Library — file guide
==================================

PHOTOS:  Copy your images here → public/media/photos/
         Example: photo-1.jpg, college-day.png

VIDEOS:  Copy your videos here → public/media/videos/
         Example: video-1.mp4, project-demo.mp4

Optional video poster (thumbnail): public/media/photos/video-1-poster.jpg

Then open src/data/mediaLibrary.ts and add/update entries with matching paths:
  src: '/media/photos/your-file.jpg'
  src: '/media/videos/your-file.mp4'
